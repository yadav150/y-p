Accessibility Tool

This tool uses your browser's built-in speech capabilities to convert speech to text and text to speech — helping those with hearing or speech impairments communicate more easily. No data is sent to any server.

Speech to Text Click the microphone, speak naturally, and watch your words appear in real-time.
Text to Speech Type or paste text, click "Speak" — and the browser reads it aloud in a clear voice.
Multi-Language Support Choose from English, Hindi, Assamese, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada.
Copy and Clear Copy your text to the clipboard or clear it instantly with one click.
Privacy First: All speech processing happens locally in your browser. No data is ever transmitted, stored, or visible to any server.
