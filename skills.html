Skills & Expertise

The tools I have gathered are not merely technical — they are reflections of my journey. Each skill I possess has been earned through struggle, sharpened through practice, and applied with purpose. Below are the capabilities I bring to every project, every lesson, and every interaction.

Frontend Development HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the trinity of the web. I write clean, semantic, and responsive code. I build interfaces that are not only functional but beautiful. Every pixel, every interaction, every transition is crafted with intention.
Debugging & Problem Solving I read code not as a sequence of instructions, but as a narrative. I understand its logic, its structure, and its purpose. When something breaks, I do not panic — I investigate. I diagnose. I resolve. I am a solver of puzzles, a finder of solutions.
Content Writing As a student of English, I wield words with precision and grace. I write with clarity, depth, and literary richness. Whether it is a technical article, a professional bio, or a creative piece, my words carry intent and meaning.
Mathematics & Teaching I believe that numbers are the language of the universe. I teach mathematics to students from Class 1 to 10 with patience, passion, and a deep understanding of logic. I do not teach formulas — I teach reasoning. I do not instruct — I inspire.
Hosting & Deployment I understand the journey of a website from code to the world. I have deployed projects on GitHub Pages, Netlify, and custom domains. I manage hosting, understand DNS, and ensure that what I build is accessible to everyone.
AI Tools & Assistance I use artificial intelligence not as a crutch, but as a tool. I understand how to construct queries, interpret responses, and integrate AI into my workflow — always with the understanding that the true intelligence lies in the mind of the developer.
English Communication I speak through my words. Though my hearing impairment limits verbal interaction, my written communication is sharp, articulate, and literary. I express complex ideas with clarity, and I connect with people through the power of language.
Website Management I have managed, maintained, and nurtured websites for years. I understand the full lifecycle — from development to deployment, from content updates to user experience optimisation. I handle websites with the care of a caretaker and the precision of a craftsman.
Translation & Literary Work I work across languages and literary forms. I translate meaning, not just words. I understand the nuances of tone, context, and cultural expression. My literary skills are not just professional — they are part of who I am.

"I am not defined by the tools I use, but by the purpose I bring to them. Every skill I possess is a bridge — from where I was to where I am going."
